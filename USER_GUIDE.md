### Quick Start

**1. Connect to the System**
- Click the **"Connect"** button in the upper right corner
- The connection will be established and you'll see welcome messages from the AI persona
- The status changes to **"Disconnect"** when the connection is active

**2. Start a Conversation**
- **Speaking**: Click the microphone button to start recording
  - The button turns red and pulses during recording
  - The AI persona responds with both voice and text

### Important Information

**Privacy & Security**
- ✓ GDPR Compliant (see badge at bottom left of chat area)
- All conversations are transmitted encrypted

**System Requirements**
- Modern web browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Microphone access required for voice functionality
- Stable internet connection recommended

**Tips for Best Results**
- Speak clearly at a normal pace
- Ask specific questions for detailed answers
- The AI persona can reference previous parts of the conversation
- You can interrupt with a new question at any time during longer responses

**Cross-Device Usage**
- **Desktop**: Full view with side AI Studio
- **Tablet**: Optimized view with slide-up AI Studio from bottom
- **Smartphone**: Touch-optimized with large buttons

### Common Actions

| Action | How To |
|--------|--------|
| Start conversation | Click "Connect" |
| Ask a question | Click microphone button → Speak → Click again |
| Interrupt response | Click microphone while voice is playing |
| Open AI Studio | Click "Open AI Studio" button (Tablet/Mobile) or automatically visible (Desktop) |
| Create summary | AI Studio → "Summary" → "Generate Summary" |
| End session | Click "Disconnect" |
